P5js Work

project 1: This audio interactive code uses the placement of your mouse to translate it into the corresponding frequency. Which you can then create intricate musical designs. When you interact please turn your volume down due to high frequencies

project 2: Snowflakes have always been a point of fascination for Sophia. Due to the short life span of these beautiful displays she created an eternal dance of snowflakes that gives a calming effect to the viewer.

project 3: Using your mouse as a brush the code takes under account the speed and direction to create a trail of red circles on the black Canvas. Showing how you always leave a footprint behind.
