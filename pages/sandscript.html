Sandscript

Sandscript is a video of a live glitch of an image that was previously glitched which you see as the final image. Sophia created this live glitch using a series of filters of the final image. This was created using a screen recorder while working in the application IbisPaintX. This is a representation of the scrambled thoughts that goes through the head of Sophia while on a road trip. The destination has since been lost but just one view of this video makes the memories begin to unscramble.
